About Sibel

Hey! I'm a junior developer based in New York. I'm currently a student at Dev Bootcamp in NYC.

I also work as a freelance artist and illustrator, book conservator, and teacher. I love to paint and draw. I find history of paper and books super interesting. I especially enjoy finding out how things work; everything I do rests on knowing how to break things down into the most basic components and building it back up again.

I have a BFA from Carnegie Mellon University, where I studied Fine Art and History. I moved to England after that and got a MA from West Dean College in Conservation of Books and Library Materials. After working in a few museums and making my way around Europe I decided to make my way back to the USA to take on my next challenge.
